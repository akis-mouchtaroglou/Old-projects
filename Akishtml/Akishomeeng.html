Akis Home


Featuring free WiFi, air conditioning, a garden and a terrace, “Akis Home” is located in Lixouri, Kefalonia.
It has sea views and provides a balcony.
"Akis Home" includes 4 bedrooms, 1 bathroom with free toiletries, a living room and a kitchen with a fridge.
The bathroom has a hairdryer and towels.
Hiking can be enjoyed nearby.
The property can accommodate up to 8 people.
